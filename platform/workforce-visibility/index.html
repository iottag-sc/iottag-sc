Understanding Workforce Activity Across The Operation

Workforces are constantly moving across complex operational environments.
People enter sites, move between work areas, interact with equipment, access restricted zones, perform scheduled works and respond to changing operational conditions.

The challenge is not simply knowing where people are

The challenge is understanding workforce activity, readiness, productivity and exposure across the operation.
