Mining Structure

Contractor

Executes daily earthmoving and extraction operations with precision, using real-time telemetry to optimize fleet utilization and payload efficiency.

Their primary focus is meeting shift targets safely while minimizing equipment wear and fuel burn

Site Management

Orchestrates multi-contractor workflows, pit designs, and processing schedules to bridge the gap between field execution and mine planning.

They rely on aggregated data to resolve immediate operational bottlenecks and ensure shift-to-shift predictability.

Corporate Leadership

Evaluates portfolio-wide asset performance, capital allocation, and long-term reserve sustainability across multiple mining assets.

They utilize high-level benchmarking data to manage market risk, report to stakeholders, and drive company-wide ESG compliance.

Tunnelling Structure

Subcontractors

Specialized teams managing critical, high-risk tasks such as shotcreting, ring excavation, or ground stabilization.

They require immediate feedback on cycle times and material yields to maintain their specific advance rates without compromising safety.

Main Contractor / Joint Venture

Oversees the complex integration of logistics, TBM operations, and multi-disciplinary subcontractors under strict contractual timelines.

They utilize cross-project analytics to mitigate risk, manage commercial claims, and keep the overall project on critical path.

Asset Owner

The public agency or private entity financing the infrastructure, focused on long-term structural integrity, lifecycle costs, and public safety.

They track macro KPIs to ensure the project delivers maximum societal value on time and within the approved budget.
