Fleet operation

Understanding Vehicle Activity Across The Operation

Vehicles and mobile equipment are constantly moving through operational environments.
Their movement influences safety, productivity, congestion, cycle times and operational performance.
The challenge is not simply knowing where vehicles are.

The challenge is understanding how fleet activity interacts with people, equipment, work areas and production activities.

iottag helps organisations create a live understanding of fleet activity, supporting safer interactions, better coordination and improved operational performance.
