By understanding the underlying critical risk factors together, organisations gain the operational insight needed to reduce exposure, improve decision-making and move towards a Zero Harm future.

Recommended Actions
Prevent Incidents From Escalating

Fatal Risk Intelligence doesn't simply identify exposure.

It helps operational leaders understand where intervention will have the greatest impact.

Examples include:

  • Restrict vehicle movements
  • Adjust traffic management
  • Delay simultaneous activities
  • Increase supervision
  • Modify work sequencing
  • Establish exclusion zones
  • Improve environmental controls
  • Initiate emergency procedures
